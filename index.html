Client Inquiries

Answers to common questions about Starlink, Electrical, and Handyman services.

1. Do you provide Starlink installation near me?
Yes. We serve the entire Ark-La-Tex region, specifically Pittsburg, Mt Pleasant, Broken Bow, and Shreveport. We offer professional, leak-proof Starlink installation for homes, ranches, and RVs.
2. My Starlink is slow or not working. Can you help?
Absolutely. We offer troubleshooting services to diagnose obstructions, cable failures, or router issues. We can often fix connection drops and speed issues in a single visit.
3. Do you do electrical repairs?
Yes. We focus strictly on repairs and troubleshooting. We can replace broken outlets, switches, and fixtures, as well as diagnose dead circuits. We do not perform heavy panel upgrades or run new service lines.
4. Can you fix my RV air conditioner or water heater?
Yes, we are mobile RV technicians. We repair AC units, water heaters, slide-outs, and 12v electrical systems right at your campsite or home.
5. Do you offer TV mounting and cable concealment?
Yes. As part of our handyman services, we professionally mount TVs and hide wires inside the wall for a clean, modern look.
6. How far do you travel?
We serve a 100-mile radius from Pittsburg, TX with NO trip fee. Beyond that, we charge a small mileage rate to cover fuel.
7. What if I need a custom mount?
We offer custom fabrication for difficult mounting situations, including gates, poles, and unique roof lines where standard kits fail.
