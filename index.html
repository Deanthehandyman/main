Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

1. Do you install Starlink in Pittsburg, TX?
Yes, we serve Pittsburg, Camp County, Mt. Pleasant, and Gilmer. We handle roof mounts, obstruction clearing, and cable management.
2. How does the Virtual Handyman service work?
It's easy. You book a time, we send a link. You click the link on your phone, point your camera at the problem (RV, electrical, etc.), and Dean guides you through the fix.
3. Can you do electrical work?
Dean has an industrial electrical background. We handle fixture replacements, outlets, troubleshooting, and RV electrical systems. For major panel upgrades, we can refer you to a Master Electrician if required by code.
4. Do you charge by the hour or by the job?
For most jobs, we give a flat bid so you know the exact price before we start. For troubleshooting or virtual calls, we have fixed hourly rates.
5. Do you work on RVs?
Yes! We specialize in RV troubleshooting, especially electrical, water heaters, and Starlink setups for mobile living.
6. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Credit Cards, PayPal, Venmo, and Cash. You can pay securely directly through this website.
7. Is your work guaranteed?
Yes. If our repair fails due to workmanship within 30 days, we come back and fix it for free. We stand by our work.
8. Do you have the right tools?
We run a fully equipped mobile shop with industrial-grade tools, ladders, and fabrication equipment. We are not just a "guy with a truck."
9. Can you help me plan a shop build?
Yes. With our fabrication and construction background, we can consult on shop layouts, materials, and cost estimation via our Virtual service.
10. How far do you travel?
We primarily serve a 50-mile radius around Pittsburg, TX. For larger jobs, we can travel further (trip charges may apply).
