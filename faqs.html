Frequently Asked Questions

Direct Answers About Our Industrial-Grade Handyman Services

Do you really travel a 200-mile radius?

Yes. Based in Pittsburg, TX (75686), we travel across East Texas and into Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Louisiana. We specialize in reaching rural locations and off-grid properties that standard local services won't cover.

How do your travel fees work?

The first 100 miles of travel are FREE. For jobs beyond 100 miles, a small trip fee is added to cover fuel and equipment transport. You can calculate your exact distance from Pittsburg on our Project Estimator.

Do you provide the Starlink hardware?

No. You must order your kit directly from Starlink.com. However, if you use our Referral Link found on our Starlink Hub, you will receive your 2nd Month of Service for FREE.

What is included in a Starlink installation?

A professional install includes secure mounting (roof, wall, or pole), industrial-grade cable routing through the structure, interior wall-plate finishing, and network optimization to ensure your Mesh system is providing maximum speed throughout the house.

What makes your service "Industrial-Grade"?

Dean’s professional background is in heavy construction, including electrical, hydraulics, pipefitting, and fabrication. We use commercial-grade tools and parts that exceed the standards of typical "big box store" hardware used by average handymen.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are a fully registered LLC and carry professional insurance. In the state of Texas, a specific "handyman license" does not exist, but we operate at a licensed-professional level in our technical trades.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major secure digital payments for your convenience, including Square, Venmo, PayPal, and Cash App. You can find direct payment links in the footer of our website.

Do you work on RVs?

Absolutely. Having lived full-time in a 40-foot Forest River Wildwood, Dean is an expert in RV connectivity and maintenance. We specialize in Starlink Mini 12V DC conversions and magnetic vehicle mounts for travelers.

Do you do emergency repairs?

While we primarily work by appointment, we do offer emergency troubleshooting for technical trades (electrical/internet) when possible. Call (281) 917-9914 for immediate availability.
