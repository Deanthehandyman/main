Common Questions

Do you really travel 200 miles?

Yes! We specialize in reaching rural homes and ranches that other services won't. Based in Pittsburg (75686), we cover a wide radius across East Texas and into neighboring states.

How do travel fees work?

The first 100 miles of travel from Pittsburg, TX are completely free. For distances beyond 100 miles, a small trip fee is added to cover fuel and time, which you can calculate using our estimator on the home page.

Are you an official Starlink employee?

No, we are independent professional installers. We specialize in the physical mounting and network integration of Starlink systems for residential and RV customers.

How do I pay for services?

We accept Square, Venmo, PayPal, and Cash App for your convenience. Payment is typically due upon completion of the job.
