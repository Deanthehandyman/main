Common Starlink Questions

Will it work at my house?

If you have a clear view of the Northern sky, yes. We use professional tools to scan for obstructions (trees, buildings) before mounting to guarantee a solid signal.

What is the monthly cost?

Standard service is typically $120/month with no contracts. Roam (RV) service is $150/month and can be paused when not in use.

Can I take it on my RV?

Absolutely. We specialize in mobile mounting solutions that allow you to have internet while moving or parked at remote campsites.

Do I need an adapter?

If you want to plug in a computer or a third-party mesh system, you will need the Starlink Ethernet Adapter. We keep these in stock for our customers!
